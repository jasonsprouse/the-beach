## Risk Analysis & Mitigation

### Risk 1: Technology Risk
**Risk:** AI models become less effective or hallucinate
**Probability:** Medium
**Impact:** High
**Mitigation:**
- Multiple model fallbacks (GPT-4, Claude, Gemini)
- Human review for critical tasks
- Continuous model evaluation
- Maintain 98%+ accuracy SLA

### Risk 2: Competitive Risk
**Risk:** GitHub/Microsoft builds similar product
**Probability:** High
**Impact:** Medium
**Mitigation:**
- Focus on specialized agents (not general purpose)
- Build deep integrations with client systems
- Create switching costs through data/history
- Move upmarket faster

### Risk 3: Market Risk
**Risk:** Market not ready for autonomous agents
**Probability:** Low
**Impact:** High
**Mitigation:**
- Start with low-risk tasks (testing, scanning)
- Prove ROI with pilot customers
- Extensive case studies
- Money-back guarantees

### Risk 4: Execution Risk
**Risk:** Can't deliver promised results
**Probability:** Medium
**Impact:** Critical
**Mitigation:**
- Conservative SLAs initially
- Over-deliver on early customers
- Build robust monitoring
- Dedicated customer success team
