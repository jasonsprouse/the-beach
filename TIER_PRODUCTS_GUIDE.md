## FAQ

### General Questions

**Q: Can I change tiers anytime?**
A: Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately, downgrades at the end of your billing cycle.

**Q: What happens to my data when I downgrade?**
A: Your data is preserved for 30 days. You'll need to reduce your usage to fit the new tier limits or upgrade again.

**Q: Do you offer custom enterprise plans?**
A: Yes! Contact sales for custom pricing, features, and SLAs tailored to your needs.

### Billing Questions

**Q: How do overages work on Basic tier?**
A: You can exceed certain quotas (agents, locations, API requests) and pay per-unit pricing. You'll be notified before charges apply.

**Q: Is there a discount for annual billing?**
A: Yes! Save $20/year on Basic (17% discount) and $100/year on Professional (17% discount).

**Q: Can I get a refund?**
A: Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans.

### Feature Questions

**Q: What's the difference between Basic and Full Lit Actions?**
A: Basic includes standard automation. Full adds priority execution, custom scripts, and advanced workflows.

**Q: Can I try Professional features on Basic tier?**
A: Some features are available as add-ons (White Label, Advanced Analytics, Priority Support).

**Q: What happens when I hit my AI interaction limit?**
A: On Freemium, requests are queued. On Basic, overage charges apply at $0.001/request. On Professional, unlimited.
